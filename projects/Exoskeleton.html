Design of Cable Driven Upper-limb Exoskeleton

Post-stroke patients suffering from motor dysfunction typically face challenges in daily activities and require rehabilitation training to regain proper muscular function. In recent decades, the use of robots to assist in recovery therapy has gained momentum. Robots can replace therapists in providing patients with diverse, repeatable, and intensive rehabilitation training. Consequently, there is a need for upper-limb exoskeletons that offer high wearability and reliability, to assist patients with effective and safe rehabilitation training.
