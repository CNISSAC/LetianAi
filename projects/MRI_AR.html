Assessment of MRI Registration with Augmented Reality

There is a high demand to develop a user-friendly and reliable assessment method that can facilitate efficient and accurate selection of MRI registration methods. By observing that MRI data is stored in a 3D volumetric format, we drew inspiration from the growing applications of Augmented Reality (AR), which has a strong capability of enabling user interaction with 3D data. Thus, we designed an AR-based system to enhance the reliability and reduce the workload of MRI registration method assessment.
